Who we are

An experienced cook, a fell runner, a forager.

FridgeAlchemy grew out of 21 years running a business, a lifetime of cooking without formal training, and the conviction that the Welsh Borders landscape holds everything you need to eat extraordinarily well. No chef’s hat. No Michelin ambitions. Just real food, honestly made, from where we are.

We run supper clubs and workshops from our Monmouthshire base, lead foraging walks in the Wye Valley and the Brecon foothills, offer running and nutrition coaching, and host wellbeing retreats in Wales and sometime further afield. Gluten-free and MCAS catering is part of everything we do, not an afterthought.

The philosophy

The fridge is already full of something worth cooking

Most people think good cooking requires expensive ingredients or specialist knowledge. FridgeAlchemy exists to disprove both. The method is about understanding what you have, knowing how flavours work together, and building the kind of larder that makes improvisation possible. No recipe required Before some evenings, we ask guests to share a picture of their fridge and a glimpse of their larder. The food that follows comes partly from what they show us — not literally, but in spirit. What you keep close is an ingredient too.

Seasonal retreats · Abruzzo, Italy

The same philosophy,
a different landscape

Each spring and autumn, FridgeAlchemy moves to a hillside retreat in Abruzzo — one of Italy’s most quietly extraordinary food landscapes. Five nights of foraging the Apennine slopes, cooking with local producers, running the mountain trails, and eating long dinners under the stars. The Welsh larder instinct, translated into Italian.
